Hiala Embotak'à Tana


About our site

Still late due to traffic jams? Hiala Embotak'à Tana (HET) is the solution. HET is a web application in which the population of Antananarivo can warn about current traffic jams. The main objectives are therefore the prevention and reporting of traffic jams that take place at such a particular time. Obviously, users are those who take the bus daily but also those who own their own cars.
